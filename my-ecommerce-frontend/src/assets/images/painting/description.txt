- 2, autumn-reflection,Wild Wings , autumn-reflection.jpg, The delicate beauty of an Autumn fog is about to disappear as the morning sun breaks through. In much the same way these deer offer the viewer only a brief glance before vanishing into the nearby woodlands.

Sculpture: 
-1, Andromeda and the Sea-Monster; Leda and the Swan, Massimiliano Soldani-Benzi, Andromeda_and_the_Sea.jpg, Each depicts an episode from classical mythology, and the two are visually linked by opposing compositions.Both figures also display heightened emotion: Leda expresses seductive eroticism, and Andromeda expresses terrifying horror.
-2, Limestone statue of a husband and wife, Found/Acquired Egypt, husband-and-wife.jpg, We do not know the names of this couple, or where their statue originate, through a number of similar statues have been found in Saqqara.
-3, Unique forms of continuity in space, Umberto Boccioni, continuity-in-space.jpg, it is a unique sculptural rhythm, not the structure of the body, but the structure of the body’s action.
-4, Forging a Sword (The Smith of Solingen), Wilhelm Albermann, smith-of-solingen.jpg, In 1895 Wilhelm Albermann’s Smith of Solingen was erected on a fountain at the market square in Solingen, Unfortunately, the fountain and the sculpture were destroyed by bombs in 1944.
